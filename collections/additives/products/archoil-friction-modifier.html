Jug of Archoil Friction Modifier

AR9100 forms a solid boundary lubricating film on friction surfaces which demostartes excellent load carrying, anti-corrosion & antiwear properties. AR9100's esters gently remove fluid system deposits while improving the antioxidative, extreme pressure & film forming properties of its host oil.

  • Reduces Engine Noise, Vibration & Friction Heat
  • Protects Engine During Cold Starts
  • Reduces wear & Extends Component Life
  • Gently Cleans Engine Deposits
  • Eliminates Ford Powerstroke 6.0L & 7.3L
